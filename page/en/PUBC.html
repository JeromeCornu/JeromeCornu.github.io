- Player Unknown’s Battle for Christmas -

Project Context

The project involves creating a Christmas-themed multiplayer game using the Unity engine. The game is developed in C# and supports local player-versus-player (PVP) gameplay.

Our Game

The game revolves around eliminating opponents to earn the maximum number of points and claim the right to receive Christmas gifts. Players take on the role of mischievous children fighting for the chance to request their desired presents for Christmas. Slide on ice and shoot at the other mischievous children to earn your gifts!
One of the players can create a session and host other players, who can join using a specific ID.

Project Creation Date

This project was completed during the fourth year at Gaming Campus, specializing in computer science with a focus on video games.

Team

The team consists of 5 developers in their fourth/fifth year at Gaming Campus.
