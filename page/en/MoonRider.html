- Moon Rider -

CONTEXT

Moon Rider is an educational game where the player must jump over obstacles at the right time to progress. It teaches mathematics to elementary school children in a fun, interactive, and progressive way. While the character moves forward automatically, a calculation appears at the bottom of the screen. The player must avoid obstacles representing incorrect results and catch the correct one.
