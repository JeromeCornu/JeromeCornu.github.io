- Boss Rush -

Project Context

The project consists of creating a vertical slice of an isometric 3D game that explores the theme of "inclusion" and aims to include as many people as possible.
A team of 6 people worked on this task over a period of 8 months.
The game can be developed using the engine of your choice.

Theme of Our Game

Our game focuses on depression. The player takes on the role of a gender-neutral middle school student who is a victim of harassment. This protagonist must confront their fears, represented as bosses. They must solve different puzzles to fight their fears in an exciting and diverse Boss Rush experience.

We wanted to explore the theme of depression through three main aspects:
  • Resistance to Others' Attacks: The player must dodge the boss's attacks without being able to counterattack, experiencing the weight of the other's attacks.
  • Thinking on Their Feet: The player must solve riddles while dodging the boss's attacks, with the riddles not intended to be difficult.
  • Revolution in the Face of the Problem: The player can attack the boss at specific moments when they take breaks in their attack pattern.
