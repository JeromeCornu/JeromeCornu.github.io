- WFC (What a Fun Creator) -

Project Context

WFC, or "What a Fun Creator", is a project focused on randomization. Randomization is the generation of unpredictable pseudo-random results with defined probabilities. The goal was to create an interface capable of displaying a randomly generated profile with numerous possible descriptions.

Each possibility varies based on previous selections. A response is then randomly chosen, taking into account its weight, ensuring a balanced and coherent experience. Everything is reproducible thanks to a Seed generated during the creation of random content. In the screenshot, you can observe white, red, and green elements, each generated entirely randomly while adhering to defined rules.
