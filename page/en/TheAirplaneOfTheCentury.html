- The Airplane Of The Century -

CONTEXT

The Airplane Of The Century is a 2D mobile bullet hell game primarily focused on performance and optimization. The game allows up to 1000 projectiles to be displayed simultaneously without any lag on mobile devices.

Project Creation Date

Created in 2024 at Gaming Campus.
