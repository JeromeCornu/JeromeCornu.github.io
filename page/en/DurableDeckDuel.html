Project Context

The project involves creating a game on the theme of sustainable development using the Unity engine. The game is developed in 2D using the C# language.

Our Game

The objective of the game is to develop doctrines to strengthen the sustainable development of our planet. These doctrines are represented by cards, each having three values representing the pillars of sustainable development: environment, economy, and social.
The game is based on choices that the player has to make to defeat their opponent.
The player can use their cards to strengthen themselves or weaken their opponent. The first participant to exceed the acceptable limits of sustainable development loses the game. You can play against an AI with an adjustable difficulty level.

Project Creation Date

The project was developed during the fourth year at Gaming Campus, majoring in computer science with a focus on video games.

Team

The team consists of four fourth-year developers at Gaming Campus.
